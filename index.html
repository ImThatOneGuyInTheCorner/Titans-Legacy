General Administrative Details

How many players can play the game?
2-4 players
What are the suggested ages for players of this game?
12+
On average, how long does it take to play a single game
There will be a scale to how long each round can be. You will decide how long this game lasts.
/w recommended settings: 45-60 minutes

Summary of the game system and objectives

How is the game played?
A settlement management game where resource gathering is done through a turn based RPG combat system
Create an outpost and expand out to other tiles
To get resources from these tiles a turn based fighting sequence will commence
If you beat the monsters on these tiles you get a certain amount of resources
What is the objective of the game?
Reach the point limit by completing objectives. (Slaying monsters, building roads, building outposts)

Components of the Game

What are the game pieces and what do they represent?
Settlement/outpost piece allows the player to attack adjacent tiles
Roads connect settlements to each other and are required to branch to other monsters
Heroes are selected at the start of the game and have certain abilities/attributes
Equipment cards can be used on Heros to make them stronger
What game specific vocabulary do players need to know?
Heroes - This is your character
Development Cards - Cards that you can craft to give you certain advantages.

Step by step of a round.

First, the players character will be displayed letting the player know it is their turn
There is a selection of actions that a player can do on their turn,

  • Build a road or outpost
  • Attack an accessible tile
  • Buy/Use Equipment Cards
  • End their turn
